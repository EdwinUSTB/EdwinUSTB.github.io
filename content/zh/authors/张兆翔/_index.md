## Biography

Prof. Zhaoxiang Zhang is a **Cheung Kong Scholar of the Ministry of Education**, **Young Top-notch Talent of the National Ten Thousand Talents Program**, and **New Century Excellent Talent of the Ministry of Education**. 

He is a researcher and PhD supervisor at the [Multimodal Artificial Intelligence Systems Laboratory](https://nlpr.ia.ac.cn/cn/index.html) and [Pattern Recognition Laboratory](http://cripac.ia.ac.cn/CN/model/index.htm) of the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He also serves as a professor at the [University of Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.ucas.ac.cn/), a core member of the [Center for Excellence in Brain Science and Intelligence Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.cebs.ac.cn/), and Executive Deputy Director of the [Pattern Recognition Laboratory, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.cripac.ia.ac.cn/CN/column/column144.shtml).

Prof. Zhang is an IEEE Senior Member, VALSE Standing AC, CCF Distinguished Member, CAAI Distinguished Member, CAAI Deputy Secretary-General, CAAI Board Member, and Secretary-General of CAAI Pattern Recognition Committee.

## Research Interests

His research focuses on:
- **Visual Cognitive Computing**: Developing computational models for visual understanding and reasoning
- **Brain-inspired Learning**: Creating learning algorithms inspired by biological neural systems  
- **Embodied Intelligence**: Advancing AI systems that can interact with physical environments
- **Visual Perception and Understanding for Open Environments**: Building robust vision systems for real-world scenarios

## Academic Impact

Prof. Zhang has made significant contributions to computer vision and artificial intelligence, with publications in top-tier venues and extensive collaborations with leading research institutions worldwide. His work bridges theoretical advances and practical applications in visual intelligence.
