Professor Zhang is a Professor and PhD Supervisor at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, and the current Director of NJU-LINK Laboratory. He conducts in-depth research in artificial intelligence, machine learning, deep learning, and knowledge graphs, has published over 50 high-quality academic papers, and has led and participated in multiple national-level research projects.

## Research Areas

- **Machine Learning & Deep Learning**: Focus on deep neural network architecture design, optimization algorithms, and theoretical analysis
- **Knowledge Graphs**: Research on knowledge extraction, knowledge fusion, knowledge reasoning, and other key technologies
- **Graph Neural Networks**: Develop novel graph neural network models for social network analysis and recommendation systems
- **Natural Language Processing**: Text understanding, information extraction, machine translation, etc.

## Major Achievements

- Published over 50 SCI/EI papers, including 20+ top-tier conference papers
- Led 3 National Natural Science Foundation projects, participated in multiple 973 and 863 projects
- Received 2 provincial and ministerial science and technology progress awards
- Supervised 15 PhD students and over 30 Master's students
